My Travel Destinations

Paris, France

The city of lights, love, and art. Paris is famous for its iconic landmarks, rich history, and delicious pastries.

Highlights:

  • Visiting the Eiffel Tower
  • Exploring the Louvre Museum
  • Walking along the Seine River

Tokyo, Japan

A perfect blend of tradition and technology. Tokyo is an exciting city full of culture, cuisine, and neon lights.

Highlights:

  • Exploring ancient temples
  • Tasting authentic sushi
  • Shopping in Shibuya

Sigiriya, Sri Lanka

Known as the Lion Rock, Sigiriya is a UNESCO World Heritage site surrounded by breathtaking landscapes.

Highlights:

  • Climbing the rock fortress
  • Exploring the gardens
  • Learning about Sri Lankan history
